Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to ImpactWednesdays ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, user ID
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, preferences
  • Contact Information: Email for communications

Location Information:

  • Approximate Location: For restaurant recommendations
  • Precise Location: For nearby restaurant search and maps

Payment Information:

  • Payment Details: Processed securely through Stripe
  • Transaction History: Booking records and receipts
  • Note: We do NOT store your full credit card numbers

Usage Information:

  • App interactions and activity
  • Restaurant views and favorites
  • Search history
  • Booking history

Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App crashes and diagnostics
  • Performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide restaurant discovery and booking services
  • Personalization: To recommend restaurants based on your preferences
  • Communication: To send booking confirmations and important updates
  • Payment Processing: To facilitate restaurant bookings
  • Improvements: To analyze app usage and improve features
  • Security: To prevent fraud and ensure account safety

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

  • Stores user data and app content
  • Privacy Policy: supabase.com/privacy

Stripe (Payment Processing)

  • Processes all payments securely
  • We do not store full card details
  • Privacy Policy: stripe.com/privacy

Google Maps (Location Services)

  • Provides maps and location features
  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

  • All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing via Stripe

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. See our Account Deletion page for instructions.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or app notification.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

Email: support@impactwednesday.com
